Refund Policy

30-Day Money Back Guarantee
We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our service.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of purchase
  • You have used less than 50% of your plan's features
  • You have not violated our terms of service
  • You provide a valid reason for the refund request

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Requests made after 30 days from purchase date
  • Accounts terminated for terms of service violations
  • Services already fully consumed or utilized
  • Third-party fees or charges

3. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@annasapota.com
  2. Provide your order number and account details
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Wait for confirmation and processing

4. Processing Time

  • Refund Approval: 2-3 business days
  • Credit Card Refunds: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal Refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer Refunds: 7-14 business days

5. Partial Refunds

For annual or biannual subscriptions, partial refunds may be calculated based on unused service time, minus a processing fee of 5%.

6. Chargeback Policy

If you initiate a chargeback instead of contacting us directly, your account will be immediately suspended and you may be liable for additional fees.

7. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Email: support@annasapota.com
  • Phone: +380 44 123 4567
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00-18:00 (EET)
